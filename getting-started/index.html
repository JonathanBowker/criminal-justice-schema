    Schema Overview

    JCS is divided into several core components that correspond to different aspects of criminal trial cases. The schema includes:

    • Case Information: Fields related to the case ID, type, jurisdiction, and status.
    • Participant Information: Information on the individuals involved in the trial, such as defendants, judges, and lawyers.
    • Evidence Metadata: Details about different types of evidence (physical, digital, testimonial).
    • Court Proceedings: Data on hearings, motions, court orders, and verdicts.

    Each section is designed to ensure that data is captured comprehensively and is compatible across systems.
